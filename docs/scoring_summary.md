# Scoring Summary

The study used a pre-test and post-test structure.

## K0 pre-test

K0 measured initial domain knowledge about public works monitoring.

The maximum K0 score was 44 points.

Corrected K0 average:

- 14.00/44
- 31.82%

## K1 post-tests

K1 was applied after exposure to the LLM-generated instructional materials.

There were two post-test versions:

- Measurement Bulletin
- Construction Monitoring Report

Each K1 version had 11 knowledge questions scored from 0 to 2.

Each K1 version had a maximum of 22 points.

Since each participant completed two K1 tests, the total K1 maximum per participant was 44 points.

Corrected K1 average:

- 41.13/44
- 93.47%

## Scoring rubric

Each K1 knowledge question was scored as follows:

- 0: incorrect, absent, or outside the domain;
- 1: partially correct, generic, or containing relevant imprecision;
- 2: correct and adequate.

## Document-level results

- Measurement Bulletin: 165/176 = 93.75%
- Construction Monitoring Report: 164/176 = 93.18%

## Condition-level results

- Condition A: 164/176 = 93.18%
- Condition B: 165/176 = 93.75%
- Difference B - A: +0.57 percentage points

## Confidence item

Each K1 version included one self-confidence item from 1 to 5.

The self-confidence question was analyzed separately and was not included in the knowledge score.

## Scope

The scores measure initial domain learning.

They do not measure the quality of participant-produced requirements artifacts, because that stage was not executed.
