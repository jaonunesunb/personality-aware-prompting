# Instruments Summary

The study used one pre-test and two post-test instruments.

## K0 pre-test

K0 measured initial familiarity with the public works monitoring domain.

It covered:

- distinction between planning and execution;
- purpose of measurement documents;
- purpose of monitoring reports;
- evidence;
- responsibilities;
- delays;
- validation rules.

Maximum score: 44 points.

## K1 post-tests

K1 was applied after exposure to the LLM-generated instructional materials.

There were two K1 versions:

- Measurement Bulletin;
- Construction Monitoring Report.

Each version had 11 knowledge questions scored from 0 to 2.

Scoring rubric:

- 0: incorrect, absent, or outside the domain;
- 1: partially correct, generic, or containing relevant imprecision;
- 2: correct and adequate.

Each K1 version had a maximum of 22 points.

Each participant completed two K1 tests, for a total maximum of 44 points.

## Confidence item

Each K1 version included one self-confidence item from 1 to 5.

This item was analyzed separately and was not included in the knowledge score.
